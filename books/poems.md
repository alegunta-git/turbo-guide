## Who am i
*Who Am I?*
*(Inner Conflict of the Mind!!)*
-------------------------
- After retirement,
- No job,
- No daily routine,
- And a quiet house.
- Which is now only,
- An echo of silence!
- I finally,
- Began to search for,
- My true self.
- Who am I?
- I built bungalows,
- Made many small and large investments.
- But, today I am confined,
- Within four walls.
- From a bicycle to a moped,
- From a moped to a bike,
- I chased speed and style,
- From a bike to a car.
- But! Now I walk slowly,
- That too alone,
- Inside my room.
- Nature smiled and asked,
- Who are you, dear friend?
- I said -
- I...! I am just me...!!
- 
- I saw states, I saw countries,
- Traveled across continents.
- But today my journey,
- Is limited from the drawing room
- To the kitchen.
- I learned cultures,
- And traditions.
- But now I only wish,
- To understand my own family.
- Nature smiled again,
- Who are you, dear friend?
- I replied,
- I...! I am just me...!!
- Once I celebrated birthdays,
- Engagements, weddings,
- With great pomp and show.
- But today,
- I count coins,
- To buy vegetables.
- Nature asked again,
- Who are you, dear friend?
- I said,
- I...! I am just me...!!
- Gold, silver, diamonds,
- Lie quietly,
- In lockers.
- Suits and blazers, untouched,
- Hang in the wardrobe!!
- But now I,
- 
- Live in soft cotton clothes,
- Simple and free.
- Once I was proficient,
- In English, French,
- And Hindi.
- Now I find peace,
- In my mother tongue.
- In the course of work,
- I traveled from country to country. Now I weigh those profits,
- and losses,
- in my memories.
- I ran a business,
- raised a family,
- made many relationships.
- But now,
- my dearest companions,
- are the affectionate elderly people,
- in my neighborhood.
- I once followed the rules,
- excelled in my studies.
- But now I understand,
- what truly matters?
- After all the ups and downs,
- of life;
- in a moment of quiet,
- my soul whispered to me—
- O traveler,
- just get ready now!
- 
- Nature gently,
- smilingly asked—
- Who are you, dear friend?
- And I replied—
- O Nature!
- You are me and I am you!!
- Once I,
- flew in the sky.
- Now I touch the earth,
- with respect.
- Forgive me,
- give me one more chance to live!
- Not as a money-making machine,
- but as a true human being,
- with values,
- with family,
- to live with love!!
- Dedicated to all retired and soon-to-be retired.
