# list of mbbs textbooks all subjects

Here is a comprehensive list of standard textbooks recommended for MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery) covering all main subjects as structured in the Indian medical curriculum. These are widely used by students and recommended by faculty across top institutions.[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5][^6]

### Anatomy
- BD Chaurasia’s Human Anatomy (multiple volumes)
- Gray’s Anatomy (reference)
- Vishram Singh’s Textbook of Anatomy


### Physiology
- Guyton and Hall Textbook of Medical Physiology
- A.K. Jain’s Textbook of Physiology
- Sembulingam’s Essentials of Medical Physiology


### Biochemistry
- U. Satyanarayana’s Textbook of Biochemistry
- Lippincott’s Illustrated Reviews Biochemistry
- Vasudevan and Sreekumari’s Textbook of Biochemistry


### Pathology
- Robbins and Cotran Pathologic Basis of Disease
- Harsh Mohan’s Textbook of Pathology


### Microbiology
- Apurba S Sastry’s Essentials of Medical Microbiology
- Ananthanarayan and Paniker’s Textbook of Microbiology


### Pharmacology
- KD Tripathi’s Essentials of Medical Pharmacology
- Rang and Dale’s Pharmacology (reference)


### Forensic Medicine
- Reddy’s Essentials of Forensic Medicine
- Parikh’s Textbook of Medical Jurisprudence


### Community Medicine
- Park’s Textbook of Preventive and Social Medicine
- Suryakantha or K Park for quick reference

### Obstetrics and Gynecology
- D.C. Dutta’s Textbook of Obstetrics
- D.C. Dutta’s Textbook of Gynecology
- Shaw’s Textbook of Gynecology
- Williams Obstetrics (reference)
  
### Ophthalmology
- Parsons’ Diseases of the Eye
- AK Khurana’s Comprehensive Ophthalmology



### ENT (Otorhinolaryngology)
- Dhingra’s Diseases of Ear, Nose, and Throat
- PL Dhingra’s Textbook of ENT
- Logan Turner’s Diseases of the Nose, Throat and Ear


### General Medicine
- Harrison’s Principles of Internal Medicine
- Davidson’s Principles and Practice of Medicine
- API Textbook of Medicine
- Macleod’s Clinical Examination (for clinical skills)

### General Surgery
- Bailey & Love’s Short Practice of Surgery
- SRB’s Manual of Surgery
- S Das Manual of Clinical Surgery
- Hamilton Bailey’s Demonstrations of Physical Signs in Clinical Surgery

### Obstetrics and Gynaecology
- D.C. Dutta’s Textbook of Obstetrics and Gynecology
- Shaw’s Textbook of Gynaecology


### Pediatrics
- OP Ghai’s Essentials of Pediatrics
- Nelson Textbook of Pediatrics (reference)
- IAP Textbook of Pediatrics

### Orthopedics
- Maheshwari’s Essential Orthopaedics
- John Ebnezar’s Textbook of Orthopaedics
- TULI’s Practical Orthopaedics


### Dermatology
- IADVL Textbook of Dermatology
- Sriram Parthasarathy’s Handbook of Dermatology


### Psychiatry
- Niraj Ahuja’s Short Textbook of Psychiatry


### Radiology
- Sutton’s Radiology and Imaging for Medical Students
- S. Sethi’s Practical Radiology


### Anesthesiology
- Basics of Anesthesia by Ronald D. Miller
- Aitkenhead’s Textbook of Anaesthesia


### Additional Reference

- Langman’s Medical Embryology (for Embryology)
- Wheater’s Functional Histology (for Histology)

This list covers all foundational and clinical disciplines for MBBS and is regularly updated as per changing medical council and university guidelines. For best results, students often supplement these with exam guides, practical manuals, and reference books as recommended by their institutions.[^3][^4][^6][^1]
